COVID-19 Safety

MASKS All guests must wear a mask at all times, and follow LA County's Mask Etiquette .
We have a strict, no mask, no service policy. If you forget your mask, don't worry, we have them for purchase for $2. Please note, you may be asked to adjust your face mask, or purchase one, to meet County requirements of proper face mask use. SOCIAL DISTANCE Check-in is now downstairs to enable 6ft distancing.
Additionally, occupancy has been reduced by half. Which means fewer people, and more one on one cat time! SANITIZE We are increasing our already strict sanitizing protocols to include sanitizing between guests visits.
