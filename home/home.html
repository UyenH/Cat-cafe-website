COVID-19 Safety

MASKS All guests must wear a mask at all times, and follow LA County's Mask Etiquette .
We have a strict, no mask, no service policy. If you forget your mask, don't worry, we have them for purchase for $2. Please note, you may be asked to adjust your face mask, or purchase one, to meet County requirements of proper face mask use. SOCIAL DISTANCE Check-in is now downstairs to enable 6ft distancing.
Additionally, occupancy has been reduced by half. Which means fewer people, and more one on one cat time! SANITIZE We are increasing our already strict sanitizing protocols to include sanitizing between guests visits.

Reservation

Due to covid-19, you can only reserve to come to the cafe. But don't worry,you can have one to one time with your favorite cat

Visit

If you are a cat lover, our cat cafe would be a heaven to. You can eat and drink while enjoy seeing cats lying around or purring around your legs. You can pet them, feed them and play them.

Safely lounge, snuggle, and socialize with a room full of 30+ adoptable cats, while supporting LAs only nonprofit cat cafe. Do Good, Feel GOOD!

Your visit directly supports our mission to reduce euthanization by increasing adoptions while socializing cats.

Adoption

If you fall in love with one of our kittos you can adopt them! We partner with Stray Cat Alliance who will finalize your adoption.
Please note, some cats are bonded or are super social and need to be adopted with a friend, or be adopted into a home with friends.
